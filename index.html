Nutmeg

Your all-in-one recipe manager, meal planner, and grocery assistant.

What Nutmeg Does

Nutmeg helps you organize every aspect of cooking and meal planning:

  • Create meal plans for any week or occasion
  • Store and categorize recipes in custom folders
  • Generate grocery lists from your meal plans or favorite recipes
  • Track pantry inventory to reduce waste and save money
  • Discover new recipes by adding URLs or subscribing to RSS feeds

Pinterest Integration

Nutmeg has a symbiotic relationship with Pinterest—any recipes you discover in-app can be pinned directly to your Pinterest boards. Simply authenticate with Pinterest, choose a board, and share your favorite recipes in one tap.

Advanced Features

  • Home screen widgets to see your next meal at a glance
  • Customizable user interface with light/dark themes
  • Additive detector and nutrition labels for healthier choices
  • Built-in calorie counter and macronutrient tracking

Get Started

Download Nutmeg from the App Store, sign in or try the free version, and start organizing your kitchen today!
